My story

I've worked at my family's restaurant for as long as I can remember. One day my father needed to update their online presence with a website and paid top dollar for the product. This spiked my curiosity and thus began my journey into software development.
I learned all the fundamentals necessary to start building static websites and my curiosity only became greater. So what began with only HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, quickly transformed into me acquiring more skills to become a Full-Stack Developer. Some of these skills include working with MongoDB and relational databases, frameworks and libraries such as React.js, Express, Bootstrap, Tailwind, etc., and using different runtime environments such as Node.
My approach to problem-solving has become more systematic thanks to the things I have been learning. This fuels me to keep studying, building,` and staying up to date on best practices to see where my skills can take me and how my approach to life and its problems evolves.
Some of my hobbies include sports, most notably soccer since I've been playing from age 5, movies, music, the great outdoors, traveling, photography, technology, and video games.
